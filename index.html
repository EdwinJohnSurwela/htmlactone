Introduction

Hello, my name is Mordecai.
I’m a tall blue jay who works as a groundskeeper at the park with my best friend Rigby.
My birthday is September 6.

I’m known for being more responsible than Rigby though I still slack off at work sometimes.
I enjoy drawing, playing video games, and hanging out with my friends.
I’ve also had relationships with Margaret the robin and CJ the cloud girl.

Even without superpowers, I’ve faced crazy adventures from battling monsters to surviving cosmic tournaments.
I always try to keep my cool and do the right thing, even when things get out of hand.

“Sometimes, you just have to take a chance and see where life goes.” – Mordecai
